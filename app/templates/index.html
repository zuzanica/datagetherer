Welcome!

This website is used to create annotations for the BUT_pedestrians dataset, which was created by students of the Faculty of Information Technology in Brno. In the "Examples" section you will find instructions on how to annotate the dataset. Once you click the start button, you will be redirected to a site where you can annotate images in the BUT_pedestrians dataset. The goal is to always choose the estimated gender, age and fashion style. The most important is your subjective opinion, the success rate is not evaluated.

About project.

The website was designed to create a new annotated BUT_Pedestrians dataset that can be used to train neural networks. The goal is to create a new dataset that will annotate the sex, age and fashion of pedestrians. Subsequently, the dataset will be used for research purposes in diploma and bachelor theses of students of the Faculty of Information Technology in Brno. The dataset is generated for research purposes and will not be published.
